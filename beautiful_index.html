Videogame 1: TANK WAR

CLICK TO DOWNLOAD THE VIDEOGAME:


This is a 3D videogame whose players can fight against each other through local area networks (LAN). In this networking videogame, there are human-controlled tanks and a machine-controlled tank that is routed by the Dijsktra algorithm which is an AI Planning technique.

In this videogame, I demonstrated my skills as a programmer given I used the knowledge learned in my University ESPOL, specially from the courses like algorithm analysis, artificial intelligence, discrete mathematics, computer networks, object-oriented programming, physics, linear algebra, and calculus. I created everything as usual, including my own communication protocol based on UDP in order to optimize the communication in real time and my own 3D render based on integer numbers, not real numbers, because using real numbers makes the 3D rendering slow.

Videogame 2: PACMAN

CLICK TO PLAY:


This is my own version of the classic videogame Pacman in which we are supposed to eat all the cookies in the maze and to avoid all the ghosts who haunt us.

All the ghosts follow predefined trajectories that trap Pacman. The red ghost is guided by an AI Planning technique which makes it more lethal. :)

Videogame 3: DUENDE

CLICK TO PLAY:


This is a videogame where you play the role of a Duende (Elf in Spanish) who must survive and fight against several enemies like ghosts, bats, piranhas, lakes and stones of lava, flames, etc. To pass to the next level, you must reach the red jar. The videogame has 21 levels. In the last level, there is a demon who spits fire. I programmed the demon with some basic AI techniques. It's an intelligent agent. The Duende is very agile. He throws boomerangs and has a parachute to fall slowly.
